Frequently Asked Questions

How do you handle unexpected costs?

We build a contingency fund (typically 5-10%) into the initial budget to cover unforeseen issues. If a change is required, we submit a Change Order for your approval before proceeding, ensuring you remain in total control of the budget.

What is the typical timeline for a commercial project?

Timelines vary based on scope and size. A standard 5,000 sq ft office fit-out typically takes 12-16 weeks. Ground-up construction can take 12-18 months. During our initial consultation, we provide a detailed critical path schedule.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Growwpark is fully licensed for general contracting in all states we operate in. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage to protect both our team and your property.

Can I live in my home during a renovation?

For minor renovations (kitchen/bath), yes. We set up dust barriers and designated work zones to minimize disruption. For whole-house renovations or major structural work, we strongly recommend finding temporary accommodation for safety and speed.

Do you offer design services?

Yes, we are a full-service Design-Build firm. This means you have a single point of contact for both architectural design and construction execution, streamlining communication and reducing errors.

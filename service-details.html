Structural Steel Fabrication & Erection

In-house design, fabrication, and erection capabilities for complex structural steel components in high-rise and industrial builds.

Scope of Work

This division manages the end-to-end process of structural steel, ensuring total quality control and efficiency. From connection design and 3D modeling to the final lift and welding on-site, our team delivers the foundational framework for large-scale, complex structures.

Key Capabilities

  • Advanced Connection Design using finite element analysis for optimized safety.
  • In-House Fabrication minimizing lead times and ensuring precise component tolerances.
  • 4D Scheduling Integration to coordinate steel delivery and erection with other trades.
  • Certified Welding Teams dedicated to highest quality standards (AWS/ISO certified).
  • Complex High-Rise Erection experienced in safely hoisting and bolting beams at extreme heights.
  • Rigorous Quality Assurance and third-party inspection processes.

Project Phases

  • Phase 1: Engineering & Connection Design 4 Weeks
  • Phase 2: Detailing & Shop Drawing 8 Weeks
  • Phase 3: Fabrication & Quality Control 12 Weeks
  • Phase 4: Site Erection & Welding Variable
